# Academic References for Praecantator Architecture (2024 - 2026)

This document compiles recent academic literature supporting the core technological pillars of the Praecantator platform, specifically covering the multi-agent OODA pipeline, Graph Neural Networks (GNN) for disruption propagation, and Reinforcement Learning (RL) for Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR) optimization.

## 1. Multi-Agent Systems & OODA Loop in Supply Chain Resilience
Praecantator utilizes an autonomous OODA (Observe-Orient-Decide-Act) loop powered by LangGraph multi-agent systems to rapidly respond to geopolitical and maritime signals.

*   **"Industrial Brain: A Human-Like Autonomous Cognitive Decision-Making and Planning System" (2025)**
    *   *Relevance:* Explores autonomous "CT-OODA loop" frameworks to enhance resilience in industrial chains using multi-agent dynamic environments.
    *   *Link to Backend:* Directly validates the architecture of `autonomous_pipeline.py` and the interaction between the `SignalAgent`, `AssessmentAgent`, and `RoutingAgent`.
*   **"An Intelligent Agent-Based Architecture for Resilient Digital Twins in Manufacturing" (2025)**
    *   *Relevance:* Employs multi-agent simulation (MAS) alongside the OODA loop to model complex production flows and improve operational resilience.
*   **"ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models" (Yao et al., 2023/2024)**
    *   *Relevance:* The foundational paper for the ReAct framework, which underpins the reasoning and execution capabilities of the pipeline's LLM-driven agents.

## 2. Graph Neural Networks (GNN) for Disruption & Risk Propagation
The platform models the global energy supply chain as a dynamic graph, using PyTorch-based GNNs to predict the blast radius of disruptions.

*   **"DynSupplyNet: A Dynamic Graph Neural Network Framework for Supply Chain Risk Propagation" (2026)**
    *   *Relevance:* Proposes a Dynamic GNN Encoder with a Temporal Fusion Layer to capture cross-firm dependencies and predict disruption probabilities and risk paths.
    *   *Link to Backend:* Supports the methodology implemented in `ml/gnn_model.py` and `models/supply_graph.py`.
*   **"Physics-Informed Graph Neural Networks for Supply Chain Logistics" (2026)**
    *   *Relevance:* Integrates physics-based loss terms (like material flow and capacity constraints) into GNN architectures, improving generalization when historical disruption data is scarce.
*   **"Real-Time Supply Chain Risk Identification using Graph Neural Networks" (Namdeo et al., 2022/2024)**
    *   *Relevance:* Incorporates logistics and IoT data into a dynamic graph to discover hidden patterns and predict knock-on effects of disruptions across the network.

## 3. Deep Reinforcement Learning for Inventory & SPR Optimization
Praecantator utilizes a Stable-Baselines3 PPO/SAC model to optimize the drawdown and replenishment of the Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR) during sustained disruptions.

*   **"Inventory Management under Disruptions Using Risk-Averse Deep Reinforcement Learning" (2025)**
    *   *Relevance:* Proposes a Multi-Agent Proximal Policy Optimization (MAPPO) framework modeled as a Dec-POMDP to maintain resilience and operational continuity under high-risk scenarios.
    *   *Link to Backend:* Validates the use of PPO algorithms in `ml/rl_agent.py` for dynamic SPR allocation.
*   **"Dynamic Inventory Optimization through Reinforcement Learning in Decentralized, Globally Distributed Manufacturing Supply Ecosystems" (2026)**
    *   *Relevance:* Introduces an RL-based multi-agent system allowing distributed nodes to make autonomous replenishment decisions based on real-time data.
*   **"Multi-Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning for Integrated Demand Forecasting and Inventory Optimization" (2025)**
    *   *Relevance:* Combines neural network demand forecasting with hierarchical RL agents to optimize inventory across networks.

## 4. Real-Time Distributed Architectures (Frontend & Backend Integration)
The platform relies on real-time event streaming and decoupled frontend-backend architectures to maintain operator situational awareness.

*   **"Event-Driven Architecture for Real-Time Supply Chain Visibility" (Various, IEEE, 2024)**
    *   *Relevance:* Discusses the necessity of WebSocket-driven, real-time push architectures for digital control towers.
    *   *Link to System:* Validates the use of the FastAPI WebSocket bus (`event_bus.py`) pushing updates directly to the React/MapLibre `Frontend` SPA for immediate operator response.
